about us

Have your break with the SEM coffee!

SEM is a recently developed coffee shop that became known in the coffee industry for it's uniqueness in 2021. It was founded by Shantal, Erraine, and Melanie. The brand is known for its innovation and quality, sourcing top-grade Arabica beans and using advanced roasting techniques in small batches. They go beyond just serving coffee, crafting an experience that transcends the ordinary. Their commitment to product promotion extends beyond serving, weaving a narrative that invites coffee enthusiasts to embark on a flavorful journey. Every cup from SEM is a testament to their dedication to the art of coffee and the essence of Philippines culture.
